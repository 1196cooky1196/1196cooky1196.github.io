I am DongYoon Shin, an incoming M.S. student in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University. I am currently completing my B.S. degree in Computer Engineering at Hongik University.

My interest in speech and audio AI grew naturally from playing in a band. Spending time performing and working closely with sound led me to wonder how voices and audio could be modeled, generated, and transformed through machine learning. This connection to sound now guides my research in text-to-speech, voice conversion, and audio generation.

My central research goal is simple: to build faster speech AI. I am interested in efficient deep learning techniques such as lightweight model design, model compression, and AutoML to make speech models faster and more practical.

I am also exploring quantum machine learning as a potential path toward accelerating model training and inference. Ultimately, I hope to connect advances in efficient and quantum computing with practical speech and audio systems.
