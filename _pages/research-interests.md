<p class="research-intro">
  My research interests focus on intelligent speech and audio systems, efficient learning methods, and emerging quantum approaches to machine learning.
</p>

      <h2>Speech &amp; Audio AI</h2>
      <p>
        I am interested in building expressive, controllable, and practical systems for speech and audio generation. My research interests include text-to-speech (TTS), voice conversion, neural audio generation, and representation learning for speech and audio.
      </p>
      <p class="research-keywords" aria-label="Research keywords">
        <span>TTS</span>
        <span>Voice Conversion</span>
        <span>Audio Generation</span>
      </p>
    </div>
  </article>

    <img src="/assets/images/efficient_deep_learning.jpg" alt="A streamlined neural network representing efficient deep learning" class="research-image" loading="lazy" decoding="async">
    <div class="research-content">
      <h2>Efficient Deep Learning</h2>
      <p>
        I study methods for making deep learning models smaller, faster, and easier to deploy. My interests include lightweight model design, model compression, pruning, quantization, knowledge distillation, and AutoML for discovering efficient architectures and optimization strategies.
      </p>
      <p class="research-keywords" aria-label="Research keywords">
        <span>Lightweight Models</span>
        <span>Model Compression</span>
        <span>AutoML</span>
      </p>
    </div>
  </article>

  <article class="research-card">
    <img src="/assets/images/quantum_machine_learning.jpg" alt="A neural network connected to a quantum computing system" class="research-image research-image--quantum" loading="lazy" decoding="async">
    <div class="research-content">
      <h2>Quantum Machine Learning</h2>
      <p>
        I am interested in quantum machine learning as a path toward accelerating computationally intensive learning tasks. In particular, I aim to explore hybrid quantum-classical methods and investigate when quantum approaches can provide meaningful speedups in model training and inference.
      </p>
      <p class="research-keywords" aria-label="Research keywords">
        <span>Quantum Acceleration</span>
        <span>Hybrid Learning</span>
        <span>Fast Training &amp; Inference</span>
      </p>
    </div>
